    ABOUT RAUNAQ

    Making top quality
    fabrication products since 1975.

    Our journey started in 1975 in Pune, Maharashtra. We provide a comprehensive range of fabrication and machined products like base frames, base plates, machine base, tanks, pressure vessels, and services for machining of all kinds of heavy components. We also render fabricated parts of railway bogies.
